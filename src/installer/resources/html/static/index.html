Welcome to the Tuakiri Discovery Service

The Discovery Service is the Gateway that lets Tuakiri users choose the home organisation they belong to, and then proceed to the log in page of their home organisation.

If you have come here in order to access a service with Tuakiri login, please have a look at our Service Catalogue, choose the service you want to access from the list, click "Open", and then log in with Tuakiri.

If you have come here to reset your selection of Home Organisation within the Tuakiri Access Federation, please go to the reset page.

More information about Tuakiri is available on our website, www.tuakiri.ac.nz.
